# Wounds & Strain

## Wounds

When you fail a **Critical Damage** save after taking **BOD** damage, you gain a **Wound** in an inventory slot. If your inventory is full, you must drop an item.

Roll on the table to determine the details of the wound, by damage type and using the amount of total damage taken to determine the _severity_ of the wound. 

### Wound table

Roll 1d8 for location if required. 

**1**: Neck, **2**: Hands, **3**: Eye, **4**: Chest, **5**: Leg, **6**: Arm, **7**: Rib, **8**: Head.

|                          |                                                                                  |                   |                 |                   |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------- | --------------- | ----------------- |
|                          |                                                                                  | **Gunshot**       | **Cutting**     | **Crushing**      |
| **1-2**<br>*Superficial* | Flesh wound.                                                                     | Graze             | Shallow cut     | Bruise            |
| **3-4**<br>*Minor*       | Pain and shock rattle you. Describe how you refocus.                             | Clean through     | Deep laceration | Hairline fracture |
| **5-6**<br>*Serious*     | _Deprived_ until you rest a few hours.                                           | Lodged projectile | Severed muscle  | Concussion        |
| **7-8**<br>*Major*       | You can barely move until you get serious help and rest.                         | Shattered bone    | Cut to the bone | Broken limb       |
| **9-10**<br>*Critical*   | A limb is missing or useless. You die within one hour without medical attention. | Destroyed limb    | Severed limb    | Mangled limb      |
| **11-12**<br>*Lethal*    | _Deprived_. You die the next time you fail a Critical Damage save.               | Massive hole      | Severed artery  | Shattered skull   |

### Wound recovery

 Your wounds heal, but they leave lasting scars. Your experience leaves you with permanent reminders of your brush with death, and you find yourself grown 
 
 After you have fully recovered and you have no Wounds remaining in your inventory, apply only the result from the most severe Wound received.


|               |                                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| *Superficial* | No effect                                                                                                                                             |
| *Minor*       | Roll 1d6; if the total is higher than your max STA take the new result.                                                                               |
| *Serious*     | Roll 1d6; 1-3 BOD, 4-6 DEX. Roll 3d6; if the total is higher than your max attribute, increase it by 1. If you roll multiple ones, reduce it by 1.    |
| *Major*       | Roll 2d6; if the total is higher than your max STA take the new result.                                                                               |
| *Critical*    | Roll 1d6; 1-3 BOD, 4-6 DEX. Roll 3d6; if the total is higher than your max attribute, take the new result. If you roll multiple ones, reduce it by 1. |
| *Lethal*      | Roll 3d6; if the total is higher than your max STA take the new result.                                                                               |




## Strain

When you fail a **Critical Stress** save after taking **CTR** damage, you gain a **Strain**. If your inventory is full, you must drop an item.

Roll on the table to determine the details of the strain, using the amount of total stress taken to determine the _severity_ of the strain.

### Strain table

|                          |                                                                                                                                                                             | Nervous / Cyberware | Cerebral / Hacks     |
| :----------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------ | :------------------- |
| **1-2**<br>*Superficial* | Agitated.                                                                                                                                                                   | Overstimulated      | Headache             |
| **3-4**<br>*Minor*       | Confusion and brain-fog. Describe how you refocus.                                                                                                                          | Emotional numbing   | Mental latency spike |
| **5-6**<br>*Serious*     | _Deprived_ until you rest a few hours.                                                                                                                                      | Tremors             | Sensory blurring     |
| **7-8**<br>*Major*       | Your system is at its limit. When using Hacks and Triggers, roll the die twice taking the higher result for yourself, and the lower result for your target if you have one. | Dissociation        | Burnout              |
| **9-10**<br>*Critical*   | Your mind begins to shut down in an attempt to save itself. Out of action and catatonic in one hour without intervention.                                                   | Neural lockdown     | Sensory blackout     |
| **11-12**<br>*Lethal*    | *Deprived*. The next time you fail a CTRL save, you either fall into a coma or enter a psychotic rage, attacking the nearest target with an Edge until stopped.             | Complete psychosis  | Neural shutdown      |

### Strain recovery

Your strain dissipates, but it leaves your mind changed. 
 
 After you have fully recovered and have no Strains remaining in your inventory, apply only the result from the most severe Strain received.

|               |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| *Superficial* | No effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| *Minor*       | Roll 1d6; if the total is higher than your max STA take the new result.                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| *Serious*     | You are becoming adapted to extreme stress. Roll 3d6; if the total is higher than your max CTR, increase it by 1. If you roll multiple ones, reduce it by 1.                                                                                                                    |
| *Major*       | You suffer from PTSD. Roll 1d6; 1-2 Fearful: you never lead the group, 3-4 Craven: your attacks are _Impaired_ unless you can see an ally, 5-6 Impulsive: you throw yourself into danger. Once treated, roll 2d6; if the total is higher than your max STA take the new result. |
| *Critical*    | An aspect of your personality changes permanently. Roll 3d6; if the total is higher than your max CTR, take the new result. If you roll multiple ones, reduce it by 1.                                                                                                          |
| *Lethal*      | Loss of humanity. When faced with a decision requiring empathy, you always make the cold and indifferent choice. Roll 3d6; if the total is higher than your max STA take the new result.                                                                                        |
