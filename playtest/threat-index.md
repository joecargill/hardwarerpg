## Aero-Drone

1 Armor, 6 BOD, 16 AGL, 10 CTR, micro-laser (d4)

- Small, cheap corporate surveillance drones equipped with lethal deterrents.
- Agile and hard to hit. Ranged attacks against them are _Impaired_.
- **Hardened**: Immune to Critical Damage while it has armor, with limit raised to 5.

## Automated Turret

4 Armor, 14 BOD, 10 CTR, heavy chaingun (d10, _blast_)

- Cannot move.
- **Hardened**: Immune to Critical Damage while it has armor, with limit raised to 5.

## Cyberpsycho

2 Armor, 1 Damping, 12 STA, 16 BOD, 14 AGL, 4 CTR, arm blades (d8+d8), redline (d6, _cyberware_) or speedware (d8, _cyberware_)

- Individuals who have pushed their nervous system past the breaking point with heavy cyberware. Highly unpredictable and violent.
- **Critical Damage**: Their attack causes massive bleeding, dealing an extra d4 BOD damage. Target is _deprived_ from blood loss.

## Corpo Operator

1 Armor, 1 Damping, 8 STA, 12 BOD, 14 AGL, 14 CTR, assault rifle (d8, _autofire_, _suppress_)

- Highly trained corporate hitmen. They use cover, suppressive fire, and tactical comms.

## Industrial Servitor

3 Armor, 16 BOD, 6 AGL, 6 CTR, hydraulic clamps (d10)

- Reprogrammed dock workers or factory lifters. Slow, hulking, and incredibly dangerous in close quarters.
- Due to their slow processing and heavy frames, attacks against targets that moved on the last turn are _Impaired_.
- **Hardened**: Immune to Critical Damage while it has armor, with limit raised to 5.

## Chimera Tank

5 Armor, 18 BOD, 8 AGL, 15 CTR, thermal beam (d12, _blast_)

- A walking tank designed for pacifying entire city blocks. Segmented architecture requires sequential destruction.
- **Hardened**: Immune to Critical Damage while it has armor, with limit raised to 5.

## Netrunner

2 Damping, 4 STA, 8 BOD, 11 AGL, 16 CTR, optic reset (d4, _hack_), overheat (d6, _hack_)

- Fragile in meat-space but deadly if they have access to the local subnet.
- **Critical Damage**: Target's optics or cyberware are hijacked. All actions are _Impaired_ until the target takes a round to reboot their system.

## Metalhead Hound

2 Armor, 12 BOD, 15 AGL, 8 CTR, titanium jaws (d8)

- Quadrupedal security robots used for perimeter defense. Fast rushers.
- Programmed for target lock. They will focus all attacks on the first person to deal damage to them.
- **Hardened**: Immune to Critical Damage while it has armor, with limit raised to 5.

## Street Ganger

4 STA, 10 BOD, 12 AGL, 9 CTR, metal pipe (d6) or pistol (d6)

- Low-level syndicate thugs or desperate locals. Rarely fight to the death unless cornered.
- **Critical Damage**: Target is mugged in the scuffle, dropping a random item.
