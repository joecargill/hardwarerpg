# Cyberware & Hacks

## Cyberware

Each individual **Cyberware** installed reduces your **max CTR** by one. You can install multiple of the same cyberware.

You may **Trigger** a bonus effect by taking stress equal to the associated die. If duration is unclear, roll the trigger die to determine number of rounds an effect lasts.

See [Marketplace > Cyberware](/playtest/marketplace/cyberware) for a list of available cyberware.


## Hacks

A **Cyberdeck** must be used to deploy a **Hack**. A **Hack Module** occupies 1 inventory slot.

Deploy a **Hack** by rolling the hack die. Take Stress equal to the result, and apply the same Stress to your target. If the Stress deals STA or CTR damage to the target, the hack is successful. Automatically successful on targets without those attributes.

If duration is unclear, roll the hack die to determine number of rounds an effect lasts.

See [Marketplace > Hack Modules](/playtest/marketplace/hack-modules) for a list of available hacks.
